Top 5 Things to Consider When Comparing Homes

1. Price vs. Value

Always look beyond the asking price. Consider the property taxes, potential renovation costs, and the local real estate market trends in that specific neighborhood.

2. Location & Neighborhood

You can change the house, but you can't change its location. Research school districts, proximity to amenities, and future development plans for the area.

3. Structural Integrity

Pay close attention to the roof, foundation, and HVAC systems. Older homes may have character, but they often come with higher maintenance requirements.

4. Resale Potential

Think about the future. Will this house be easy to sell in 5 or 10 years? Features like extra bedrooms or a finished basement can significantly impact future value.
